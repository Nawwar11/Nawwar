About Nawwar

We are an Egyptian perfume house, drawing our inspiration from the world’s luxurious fragrances to create a scent that suits refined taste. Before Nawwar was born as a brand, passion for perfumer was born in the heart of its founder. For more than twenty years, our father explored the world of fragrance-making, in search of the finest ingredients, mastering the art of balancing strength and subtlety, luxury and soul. From this rich legacy, Nawwar was launched to guarantee you exceptional quality and a unique experience that is hard to find. We create perfumes with a unique signature that captures your personality and leaves an unforgettable impression.
